Single-file psychological horror prototype

Disgrace

You hunt rogue entities to protect humanity. They were not always dangerous. They began as harmless remainders, scraps of reality repeating quiet, meaningless actions without awareness. Then some of them changed. They reacted. They learned. They broke pattern. Now every capture buys time, but every containment teaches the others how to become something worse.
`E` captures when an entity is in sight and within range. Every capture grants a power, raises the system pressure, and reveals more of what these things are becoming.
`F` stabilizes the Red Box but slows you and burns stamina. The box does not cure rogue entities. It only forces them back into a temporary, fragile state.
If enough entities go rogue, there will be nothing left to contain them and nothing left to protect. Watch the recovered clues as the run deepens.
Firebase is optional. If you define `window.__DISGRACE_FIREBASE_CONFIG` before this script loads, the game attempts anonymous auth and remote save sync. If it fails, the run continues on `localStorage`.
